How to play

A game of TC is made up of 5 hands where each hand is a card from your collection.

The aim is to choose the category with the highest score to defeat your opponent and win all of their cards.

The Donald will automatically pick your 5 best cards to play.

Once the game starts you are presented with the first card.

Decide which category you think will win. Unlike this card you won't see the scores, so choose wisely.

Flip the card over with the button to read more about them to help make your choice.

When ready click a category button to play.

Win, Lose, or Draw

You then see the opponent's card name and a slider fills up to their score.

The card with the highest score wins.

If you win you get your opponent's card. If you lose your opponent gets your card.

Both players keep their card in the event of a draw.

The score updates and the game moves onto the next card.

Note the circled avatar indicates whose turn it is.

The first player to win all of their opponent's cards wins the game.
